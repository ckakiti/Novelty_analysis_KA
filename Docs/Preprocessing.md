## Raw data structure
```
.
+-- Group_name
|   +-- animal1_name
|       +-- date1
|           +-- session_date
|               +-- depth.dat
|               +-- depth_ts.txt
|               +-- metadata.json
|               +-- rgb.mp4
|               +-- rgb_ts.txt
|       +-- date2
|       +-- date3
|   +-- animal2_name
|   +-- animal3_name
```
